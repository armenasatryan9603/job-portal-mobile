Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how HopWork uses cookies and similar technologies in our mobile app and on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, understand how you use HopWork, and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies – required for core functionality and security.
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand usage patterns and improve the app.
  • Preference cookies – remember your settings (for example, language).
  • Advertising cookies – used by our ad partners to show relevant ads.

3. Third-Party Technologies

We may use third-party services such as analytics providers and ad networks. These providers may set their own cookies or use device identifiers according to their privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies and tracking technologies through your browser or device settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of HopWork.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact

For questions about cookies or privacy, please contact us through the support options in the app or on the HopWork website.
