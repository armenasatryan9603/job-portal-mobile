GDPR Compliance

Last updated: February 2025

If you are located in the European Union (EU), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal data. This page summarizes how HopWork addresses those rights.

1. Legal Bases for Processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following:

  • Performance of a contract (providing our services to you).
  • Your consent (for example, for marketing or certain analytics).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Our legitimate interests (such as improving and protecting HopWork).

2. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data, in certain circumstances.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data, in certain circumstances.
  • Request data portability, where technically feasible.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent.

3. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

4. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the EU/EEA. When we do so, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to protect your information.

5. Contact and Complaints

To exercise any of your rights or to ask questions about GDPR, please contact us via the support options in the app or on the HopWork website. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
